18th CENTURY GOWN

A 1770's English polonaise gown modeled on Janet Arnold's Patterns of Fashion gown rendered in 1/2 scale.
Designed as the hero gown for an adaptation of Cinderella. Underskirt made of golden satin, overskirt and bodice made of red brocade and lace.

A plush of Peppermint Butler, a character from Adventure Time. Made of recycled textiles and found materials. Cotton, satin, polyfill.
